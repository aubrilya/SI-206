Welcome!

Founded in 1986, West Michigan Plastics, Inc. is a custom injection molder of engineered thermoplastic resins, located in Holland, Michigan. Clean and well organized, this facility provides every employee with a safe, efficient workplace to ensure quality throughout the entire manufacturing process. We service a range of industries, including:

  • Automotive
  • Furniture
  • Medical
  • Agricultural
